## Inputs
- **Video to extend** (Video): The source video to extend.

- **Negative Prompt** (Text): What to avoid in the generation.

## Outputs
- **Extended Video** (Video): The resulting extended video.

- **Model**: veo-3.1-generate-preview
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 720p
- **Duration**: 8 seconds
