**Category**: AI
**Subcategory**: Video

Generate videos from text prompts.

## Inputs
- **Prompt** (Text): Description of the video.
- **Negative Prompt** (Text): What to avoid.

## Outputs
- **Output** (Video): The generated video.

## Configuration
- **Model**: veo-3.1-generate-preview
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 720p
- **Duration**: 8 seconds
